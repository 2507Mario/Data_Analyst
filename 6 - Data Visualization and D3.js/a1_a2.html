Iteration 1
Udacity´s Project 6 - Visualization

Originally, we wanted to do this project showing together 2 plots, and comparing them: the first one was the Life Expectancy by year and the second one the Health Expenditure by year. Both were produced by region (Europe & Central Asia, North America...etc). We produced the graphics A1 and A2

PLOT A2: health expenditure for the different world regions, from 1995 to 2010:

Feedback


Some colleagues, friends and relatives were asked about this first sketch of the visualization:


  • Not clear what the region grouping refers to: all people asked where confused about which countries were included in which region. The had a vague idea, but that was not enough. For instance, they all included countries from Panama to north in "North America". In fact, only the United States and Canada are included in this category.

  • Life Expectancy lines too close to each other: recommendation to "shrink" the Life Expectancy axis, starting in 40 or so.

  • The legend on top overlaps the graph.

  • The second graphic is better, still the region and the overlapping legend problem.

  • Difficult to compare the plots because the colors of the regions (legend) do not match.

  • Second plot, left axis legend unreadable.

Bottom line recommendation: before doing anything, try with countries instead of regions to see if we can get a better idea.
