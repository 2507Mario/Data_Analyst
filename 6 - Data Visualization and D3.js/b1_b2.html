Feedback


When asked again they answered:


  • Now, the countries are clear, however, there are too many, rendering the visualization "messy" to observe.

  • The rest of the problems expressed in the first iteration are of course there: Life Expectancy lines too close to each other: recommendation to "shrink" the Life Expectancy axis, starting this time in 50 or so. The legend on top overlaps the graph.

  • The second graphic is better, still the region and the overlapping legend problem plus the left axis legend is unreadable.

  • Difficult to compare because the colors of the regions (legend) do not match.

Bottom line recommendation: this does not work well. One of the reviewers pointed out that it could work by combining a histogram with a line: a histogram could show the average expenditure by country and the line the average life expectancy, in this way all the information is in the same graphic and therefore is easier to compare. Well, we will give it a try.
